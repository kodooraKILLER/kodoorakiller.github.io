NVOK - the quiz program

C++

Developed in 2017, its primary goal was to replicate the KBC reality show's quizzing system. The project further won the school's science exhibition. The program takes 15 questions from a pool of 100+ questions in a random manner, ranging from easy to hard, and proceeds till the last question provided the previous question was correctly answered.

Completed and deployed

Offline Elections

C++

Developed in 2018, its primary goal was to conduct school elections. 3 developers took part in the building of this project at a timespan of just 1 month, under the guidance ofone faculty. The program conducts school elections in an interactive way, with loading bars and beep sounds after every vote has been polled. results view was secured with a password, which only the principal may access.

Completed and deployed
